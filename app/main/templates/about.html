Who We Are: Redefining Medical Billing

At Ardur Healthcare, we are redefining how healthcare providers experience medical billing and coding. Founded in 2023, we envisioned a smarter, high-quality, technology-enabled Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) solution for medical practices across the United States.

We recognized early that providers were burdened by outdated systems, claim delays, and revenue leaks. By combining deep industry knowledge with intelligent automation, Ardur Healthcare has emerged as a trusted partner for practices seeking results-driven billing operations.
