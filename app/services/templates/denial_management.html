Common Denial Types We Handle

Our expertise covers all types of claim denials.

Missing Information

Claims denied due to incomplete or missing patient or provider information.

Eligibility Issues

Claims denied due to patient eligibility, coverage, or benefit limitations.

Coding Errors

Claims denied due to incorrect, missing, or incompatible procedure or diagnosis codes.

Timely Filing

Claims denied due to submission after the payer's filing deadline.

Authorization Issues

Claims denied due to missing, invalid, or expired prior authorizations.

Medical Necessity

Claims denied because the payer determined services were not medically necessary.
